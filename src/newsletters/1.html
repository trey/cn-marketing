Welcome to Cassette Nest!


TL;DR:
Cassette Nest is an app to track your film photography. It's in active development and is a little rough around the edges. Create an account here and give me feedback via Twitter or Slack .
Create an account
I’ve been building this in my nights and weekends ( and recently even more often ) starting about a year and a half ago. Things are still a bit raw, but there are a handful of folks using the app now who are giving me feedback. Join us if you want to help shape its direction!
The Homepage
This will be a subscription-based service, but everything is open and free as it's being developed.
Film Inventory Page
The original idea of this app was to replace both a spreadsheet and Trello board that I was using to track film. I was using Trello to keep up with inventory and a Google Sheet to track film once it was loaded, shot, developed, and eventually archived in a binder on my shelf.
A handy way to see everything ready to develop.
My hope is that Cassette Nest will make it easy and fun to build a lifelong archive of metadata about your film photography. You can read even more about the philosophy behind this project on my blog .
Logbook page showing a sampling all your beautiful metadata.
