Happy end of the year! I hope everybody is safe and well. Please wear a mask whenever you leave home and get a COVID-19 vaccine whenever it’s available to you. Pretty please!

Since last we spoke …

  • We now have a public changelog. If you want to stay up to date beyond what’s in these emails, that’s a good place to look. There’s even an RSS feed if that’s your thing.
  • You can now export all your own data! Importing those exports needs streamlining before being released to the public, but we’ll get there.
  • The navigation is better. Let me know what you think!

Other stuff that happened this year.

  • You can add your own film and film manufacturers. You can use them right away but they can potentially be adjusted and made available to everybody. Thanks for helping out!
  • You can create cameras that allow for multiple camera backs. This allows for a little bonus of being able to load 35mm film in a medium format camera (if you define your camera back that way).
  • Cassette Nest was featured on Nico’s Photo News. I think this is how a lot of you arrived here. Glad to have you!

That’s all for today. As always, I appreciate every one of you and I love getting feedback, so have at it!

Later,
